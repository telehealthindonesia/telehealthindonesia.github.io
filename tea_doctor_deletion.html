Account Deletion Instructions

To delete your TEA Doctor App account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open your prefered email client.
  2. Send us below email
  3. Set the email subject to: TEA Doctor Account Deletion Request
  4. In the email body, please include the following information:
    • Your account/email: [Your Account/Email]
    • Your reason for account deletion (optional): [Reason for Deletion]
  5. Send the email to: firebase.tsi@gmail.com

Your account deletion request will be processed by our team. Please allow some time for the request to be completed. You will receive a confirmation email once the account deletion is finalized. Please note that this action is irreversible and you won't be able to recover your account.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact our support team at firebase.tsi@gmail.com.
